My Story

Hi, my name is Tokollo Simbini, an unemployed Electrical Engineering graduate. After finishing my degree, I realized how tough it is for graduates with no experience to find real opportunities. I created GraduateInHunt to connect graduates like myself with recruiters willing to give us a chance through internships, learnerships, and entry-level positions. This platform is built to inspire hope and create a bridge between education and employment.
